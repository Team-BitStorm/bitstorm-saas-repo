# BitHealth

**Getting the right care at home — without the search.**

Finding a qualified therapist, nurse, or specialist who travels to your home is harder than it should be — especially for elderly patients, people with limited mobility, or anyone navigating a complex medical situation.

BitHealth is a marketplace that changes that. Healthcare providers list their availability, the services they offer, and how far they are willing to travel. Patients search by location, pick an open time slot, and confirm a home visit — all in one place.

## How it works

1. **Create an account** — sign up as a patient or a healthcare provider.
2. **Find a provider near you** — browse by service, availability, and distance from your home.
3. **Book a visit** — choose a slot that works for you; the provider comes to you.

## For providers

Manage your weekly schedule, set your own prices per service, define your travel radius, and receive booking requests directly through the platform.

## Built with

React · Django · PostgreSQL
